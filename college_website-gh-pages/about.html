About us

The ICMS college Timergra for Boys and Girls ,A place of disciplined and Quality Education

International college of modern sciences (ICMS) - is established to cater to the emerging needs and opportunities and meet economic challenges being thrown by the incessant IT revolution. It is setup as a Public Private Partnership (PPP) model. It is a major initiative taken by the indivisioval persons not govt. thay support to the Institute by providing 50 acres of land and Building. It is aimed at providing future candidates who shall become a precursor in the field of IT and Sciences. A Governing Council consisting of distinguished people from academia, industry and government preside over the governance of the institute.

The Institute aims at evolving strong research programmes in a plethora of areas where IT shall provide a common link with profound emphasis on development of technology and its application.
